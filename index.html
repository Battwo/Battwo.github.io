Hey, My name is Alexanny Nunez! I’m a senior at Brooklyn STEAM Center, and I’m all about full-stack development right now. I’ve learned a bunch of languages like JavaScript, Python, and HTML/CSS, and I’m getting into Tailwind CSS too. One of the projects I’m proud of is Eco Bloom, a business website I worked on with my team. I did most of the design work, including the landing page and the survey page.
