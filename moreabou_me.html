◊Eco Bloom ! ◊

Eco bloom was a collaborative project me and my teammates made together! Eco bloom is a business that aims to reduce plastic waste by upcycling plastic materials or any waste, and turning them into accessorized jewelry to wear or even as household objects t just have around the house. Eco Bloom’s mission is to reduce plastic waste in New York by 25% within our first year of business, starting with these underserved areas. Through upcycling locally collected trash, we aim to turn waste into opportunity — creating jobs, cleaner streets, and a model for sustainable change rooted in environmental justice.
